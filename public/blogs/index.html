Insight Genesis Wins Prestigious Global-AI Decentralized Intelligence Award

HONG KONG, February 27, 2025 — Insight Genesis, a web3 subsidiary of Insight Genie, has been awarded the coveted Global-AI Decentralized Intelligence Award at the Global-AI Award 2025 ceremony held at Exchange Square, Hong Kong.

The prestigious event, hosted by CSDN Global and Hashkey in partnership with AGI Open Network, recognized Insight Genesis for its exceptional innovation in integrating artificial intelligence with blockchain technology.

“This recognition validates our vision of bridging advanced AI solutions with decentralized technology,” said Branson Lee, CEO & Co-Founder at Insight Genesis. “We’re honored to receive this award and remain committed to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible at the intersection of AI and blockchain.”

About Insight Genesis

Insight Genesis, launched in 2025 as the web3 subsidiary of Insight Genie, combines advanced AI capabilities with blockchain technology to revolutionize data verification and analysis across multiple sectors. Backed by HSBC and other prominent investors, the company leverages facial recognition, voice analysis, and digital footprint data to provide predictive insights with up to 92% accuracy.

Key Sectors

  • Financial Inclusion: Revolutionizing access to financial services through decentralized risk assessment for the unbanked market.
  • Wellness & Health: Transforming healthcare delivery with cryptographically secured health insights via blockchain.
  • Education: Enabling prescriptive educational guidance through decentralized learning and career analytics.
  • Human Resource: Reinventing talent management with transparent, AI-powered verification systems.

About the Global-AI Award

The Global-AI Award, organized by CSDN Global and Hashkey in partnership with AGI Open Network, celebrates excellence in the integration of AI and blockchain technology. The Decentralized Intelligence Award was determined through a rigorous evaluation by industry experts, focusing on innovation, practical application, and industry impact.

The award ceremony featured expert panel discussions on AI and Web3 trends, with a focus on Agent Ecosystem development and AI-powered blockchain applications.
